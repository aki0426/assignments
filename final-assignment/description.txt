This webpage is for the fictional city of Waterdeep located within the library network of Forgotten Realms.
The site is simple and has the basic home page displaying the latest news bulletin or message from the library.
The menu is located right under the header for easy accessibility and directs to About Us, library Services, Calendar or Events, the Catalog, and the user Account pages.
At the bottom footer includes the Locations of the Waterdeep branch and links to other branches, Hours, and a search the website function.
